MealMind

MealMind is thoughtfully designed to build an AI-driven nutritional recommendation system that recommends personalized meal ideas based on the available ingredients at the user's home. It details how much of each ingredient is required, along with step-by-step preparation instructions. The project helps to provide the estimated number of servings for each meal. The main aim is to reduce food waste, promote balanced nutrition, and simplify the meal-planning process through an intelligent, user-friendly application.
